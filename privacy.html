Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 8, 2025

At Pluto’s Games, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle data in relation to our mobile game(s), including Tri-Merge Cascade, and any interactions you have on our website.

1. What We Collect

  • Anonymous gameplay data (e.g. high scores, level progress)
  • Device type and OS version (for performance optimization)
  • Crash reports and bug logs

We do not collect or store:

  • Personal information such as your name, address, phone number, or email
  • Financial or payment information (in-app purchases are handled by app stores)

2. Why We Collect It

  • Improve gameplay balance and performance
  • Fix bugs and crashes
  • Understand how players interact with our games to improve experience

3. Third-Party Services

We may use services like Unity Analytics or Firebase to collect data anonymously. Ad providers like AdMob may also gather limited data to serve relevant ads.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your data for marketing. We share data only with the services mentioned above or if legally required.

5. Children’s Privacy

Our games are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

6. Contact

Questions? Contact us at privacy@plutosgames.com
